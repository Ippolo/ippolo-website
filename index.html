ABOUT ME

IPPOLO is an electronic music producer based in Minsk, Belarus. With roots tracing back to the vibrant club scene of the 90s, he has evolved a unique style that blends Organic House, Progressive, and Trance.

Inspired by legends like Jean-Michel Jarre, Kraftwerk, and ATB, his tracks have reached the Top 100 on Beatport (Organic House).

"I believe that every person is a whole universe. My mission is to create a cozy, safe world around you through sound - a place without problems, wars, or conflicts."
